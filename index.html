ISLA DEL FUEGO

Known for being one of the best, if not the best, beach in the Philippines. It’s a bright, sunny island bursting with natural beauty everywhere you look.

Located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines, this hidden gem is a paradise for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking to experience a unique and tranquil island getaway.

Experience Siquijor

Siquijor is a fantastic destination for adventure seekers, beach lovers, and cultural enthusiasts. Here are some of the top things to do on the island:

Good Food

Siquijor is not just about adventure; it’s also home to delicious local cuisine.

Worldwide Recognition

Siquijor is safe and welcoming for tourists all around the world. The locals are friendly, and the island has a laid-back, peaceful atmosphere.

Accessible, from anywhere.

The island has a small Siquijor Airport, but most tourists arrive via Dumaguete-Sibulan Airport, which has flights from Manila and Cebu.

Beach Ambience

Siquijor is home to some of the most stunning and untouched beaches in the Philippines.

Mountain on an Island?

Mt. Bandilaan National Park – The highest point in Siquijor, offering panoramic views and a chance to visit healing rituals.

Activities

Our little island can provide huge range of activities. From island-hopping, to hot air baloon rides.
